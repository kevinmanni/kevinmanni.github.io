Introduction

Fantasivärld

For my website, I'm creating a music publication website called Fantasivärld, which means "fantasy world" in Swedish. The site focuses on a niche but growing corner of the music scene: artists who make calm, ethereal, and emotionally immersive music. This includes musicians like Bladee, Ecco2k, Caroline Polachek, Aphex Twin, Boards of Canada, Ethel Cain, and much more. These artists share a dreamy, otherworldly quality in their sound and aesthetic. They blend genres like dream pop, ambient trap, and experimental art pop, often evoking feelings of detachment, longing, or spiritual escapism. The name Fantasivärd references this surreal atmosphere and is also a nod to Bladee’s Swedish roots. The website will feature in-depth artist spotlights/interviews, curated Spotify playlists, as well as, essays and reviews of new and old music.
