About Us

Welcome to Nuovo Vesuvio, where the timeless flavors of Italy meet the warmth and hospitality of family tradition!

Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of the Amalfi Coast, our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you authentic Italian dishes crafted with the finest, locally sourced ingredients.

Whether you're savoring our signature wood-fired pizzas, indulging in house-made pastas, or enjoying one of our carefully curated pastas, every meal is designed to transport you to the heart of Italy.

At Nuovo Vesuvio, we believe that dining is not just about food—it’s about creating memorable experiences. Our welcoming atmosphere, attentive service, and commitment to quality make us the perfect place for both intimate dinners and celebratory gatherings. Join us and discover why we’re more than just a restaurant—we’re a celebration of Italian culture, family, and the joy of good food shared with good company.
